# Workshop Proposal: Inclusive AI & Amharic NLP (Addis Ababa)
**Theme**: Bridging the Gap between Frontier AI and Local Linguistic Sovereignty.
**Target Audience**: Local developers, NGO leaders, linguistic students, and policy makers.

## 📅 Event Concept: "Qwen for Ethiopia"
A half-day seminar and hands-on workshop focused on using Qwen models to build culturally resonant applications for the Ethiopian market.

## 🎯 Core Objectives:
**Awareness**: Introduce the Qwen-72B and Qwen-Audio capabilities to the local tech ecosystem.

**Technical Training**: Workshop on "Amharic Prompt Engineering" using the recipes developed in this repository.

**Bias Dialogue**: A roundtable discussion on mitigating gender and financial bias in local AI implementations.

## 📍 Proposed Logistics:
**Location**: Addis Ababa (Tech hub or University partner).

**Format**: Hybrid (In-person + Live Stream for regional reach).

**Partnership**: Seeking collaboration with the local developer community and academic institutions.

## 🚀 Potential Impact:
By hosting this event, we establish a direct feedback loop between Ethiopian users and the Qwen development team, ensuring that the next generation of models is better equipped for the Horn of Africa.

**Status**: Planning Phase - Seeking Qwen Ambassador Program support for Q3 2026.
