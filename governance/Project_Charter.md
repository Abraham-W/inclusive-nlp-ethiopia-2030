# Project Charter: Inclusive-NLP-Ethiopia-2030

## 1. Vision Statement
To ensure that Ethiopian AI systems act as catalysts for equity by eliminating linguistic and gender biases in Amharic Large Language Models (LLMs).

## 2. Problem Statement
Current LLM training data for Amharic is heavily skewed toward masculine-as-neutral morphological forms, particularly in professional and financial contexts. This creates a "digital glass ceiling" for female professionals in automated systems.

## 3. SMART Objectives (Q2 - Q4 2026)

* **The Gold-Standard Dataset (Technical):** * **Goal:** Curate and release the `Amharic-Finance-Inclusive-v1` dataset.
    * **Metric:** 500+ verified prompt-response pairs targeting gender-neutral financial terminology.
    * **DE2030 Link:** Supports "Inclusive Digital Economy and Fintech."

* **The "Linguistic Sovereignty" Report (Research):** * **Goal:** Publish a comparative evaluation report: *“Qwen-72B vs. Legacy LLMs in Amharic Morphological Accuracy.”*
    * **Metric:** A formal whitepaper hosted on GitHub.
    * **DE2030 Link:** Promotes "Digital Sovereignty and Local Data."

* **Community Capacity Building (Events):** * **Goal:** Execute the **"Inclusive AI Addis"** workshop series.
    * **Metric:** Engage 50+ local developers and 20+ NGO/Gov decision-makers in 2 high-impact events.
    * **DE2030 Link:** Directly supports "Innovation and Youth Skills."

* **The Open-Source "Bias Auditor" (Tools):** * **Goal:** Launch a Python-based utility for localizing bias detection.
    * **Metric:** Secure 10+ contributions/stars from the **Masakhane** or local developer community.
    * **DE2030 Link:** Encourages "Responsible AI Governance.

## 4. Strategic Alignment
* **National Vision:** Digital Ethiopia 2030 (Digital Infrastructure & Inclusion).
* **International Standards:** Alignment with UNESCO’s Recommendation on the Ethics of Artificial Intelligence.
